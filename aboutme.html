I'm driven by curiosity!

Data analyst with a vocation as a communicator. After five years in advertising, I found myself in data analysis, a field of questioners. The beauty behind data and how it can contribute to effective results in all types of corporations has always caught my eye.

In addition, as a communicator, storytelling, business analysis, teamwork, negotiating demands and coordinating projects are strengths I have acquired throughout my career.

I believe that data analysis instigates one of the most wonderful things in life: curiosity. That's why I seek knowledge about the area every day, as it's something that moves me according to my purpose.
