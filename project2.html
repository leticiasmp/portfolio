PowerBI Practices

The Data Science Academy's Microsoft Power BI aims to improve knowledge of the Microsoft Power BI tool and also to apply Data Science techniques to generate predictive models and extract insights for decision-making, integrating Power BI with R, DAX and Python.

In this course, I developed some practice projects within the tool, see below:

Marketing campaign analysis

The project aims to illustrate metrics regarding customer vision, customer buying behavior, marketing campaign performance and buying patterns at the point of sale. To do this, we cleaned the data by removing outliers through the use of scatter plots and also performed calculations to extract sales data relevant to our analysis.

Commercial Dashboard - Sales Performance

In the project, I used metrics such as sales volume, average ticket, conversion rate, sales cycle, customer retention, among others, to present the current scenario of the sales team and its respective results.

×

Logistics data analysis

The activity sought to develop a dashboard that would provide an understanding of the company's product delivery process. To do this, data was used on total deliveries per channel, percentage of early deliveries per team, deliveries per month, salespeople with the highest number of deliveries and percentage of delivery statuses.
